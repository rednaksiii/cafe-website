Green tea


Green tea is a type of tea that is made from Camellia sinensis leaves and buds. It originated in China and has been enjoyed for its taste and health benefits for centuries. Green tea is minimally processed, which helps retain its natural nutrients and antioxidants. It contains caffeine, although typically in lower amounts than coffee, and has a range of potential health benefits, such as boosting metabolism, improving brain function, and reducing the risk of certain diseases. Green tea can be enjoyed hot or cold, and there are many varieties and flavors available to suit different tastes.
